About Pishing(Potential Security Threats)

'Pishing' is a common form of internet privacy. It is deployed to steal users personal and confidential information like bank account numbers, net banking passwords,credit card numbers, personal identity details etc.Later the perpetrators may use the information for siphoning money from the victim's or run up bills on victim's credit cards. In the worst case one could also become the victims identity theft.A few customers of some other Indian banks have been affected by the attempt of pishing in early 2006


We would like to be aware of methodologies in a 'Phishing' attack,do's and dont's in sharing of personal information and the action to be taken in case you fall prey to a pishing attemp

Methodologies

  • Phishing attacks use both social engineering and technical subterfuge to steal customers personal identity data and financial account credentials
  • Customer receives a fradulent mail seemingly from a legitimate internet address.
  • The email invites the customer to click on a hyperling provided in the mail
  • Click on the hyperlink directs the customer to a fake web site that looks similar to the genuine site
  • Usually the email will either ise a reward on compliance or warn of an impending penality on a non comilance.
  • Customer is asked to update his personal information, such as passwords and credit card and bank account numbers etc.
  • Customer provides personal details in good faith.Clicks on 'submit' button
  • He gets an error page.
  • Customer falis prey to the phishing attempt
